# 🌹 Dusk Rose Farm

**Dusk Rose Farm** is a small, family-run farm based in **Mooresville, North Carolina**.

This site exists to share a simple, clear overview of who we are, what animals we raise, and when we have animals available for new homes. It’s designed to be clean, easy to navigate, and focused on real farm life — not overly commercial, not complicated.

We believe in:

* Raising animals with care and consistency
* Honest communication
* Matching animals with the right homes
* Keeping things simple and local

---

## 🐐 What You’ll Find Here

* An overview of the animals we raise
* Current availability updates
* Contact information
* Links to our Facebook and Instagram for daily photos and farm updates
* Access to our Square store when applicable

Our social media pages — especially Instagram — show more of the day-to-day moments, cute photos, and seasonal updates.

---

## 🏡 About the Farm

Dusk Rose Farm is centered around practical, small-scale farming.
Availability changes seasonally, and not all animals are available at all times.

When animals are listed as available, we prioritize good matches and responsible placement.
